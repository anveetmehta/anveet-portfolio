# PRD v1 — AnveetMehta.com
## Building a Living System of Thought, Operations, and Intelligence

---

## 1. Executive Summary

AnveetMehta.com will evolve from a personal portfolio website into a living operating system for:
- systems thinking
- operational intelligence
- AI-native workflows
- trust infrastructure
- entrepreneurial exploration

The website should NOT feel like a resume, consulting website, startup landing page, or traditional personal brand site.

Instead, it should feel like: **entering the mind of a systems builder.**

The experience should communicate:
- intellectual depth
- operational understanding
- emerging founder energy
- a coherent worldview around modern digital systems

---

## 2. Core Vision

The future of digital products is shifting from interfaces to intelligent operational systems.

Modern organizations increasingly depend on:
- onboarding systems
- trust infrastructure
- workflow orchestration
- machine-assisted operations
- AI-supported decisions
- operational cognition

This website becomes the public operating layer of that thinking.

**Objective:** Create immediate intrigue + clarity.

The experience should: establish tone · create curiosity · avoid overexplaining · communicate systems-oriented thinking.

**Intro Text:** AI • Systems • Trust Infrastructure

**Main Headline (Preferred):**
> Designing systems for how modern digital trust operates.

**Alternative:**
> Exploring the intersection of AI, operations, and trust at scale.

**Supporting Copy:**
> Most products focus on interfaces.

## 17. Content Engine Workflow

### Phase 1 — Discovery
Sources: Reddit · Hacker News · X/Twitter · LinkedIn · RBI · FIU · SEBI · fintech news · AI workflow discussions

### Phase 2 — Classification
Topics classified into: operational intelligence · AI workflows · onboarding systems · fintech infrastructure · trust systems · compliance intelligence · decision systems

### Phase 3 — Opportunity Scoring
Each topic receives: trend score · strategic alignment score · SEO score · founder narrative score · contrarian insight score

### Phase 4 — AI-Assisted Writing
Generate: article structures · thesis ideas · hooks · outlines · metadata · social snippets

### Phase 5 — Human Intelligence Layer
AI assists. Human thinking leads. The platform must avoid: generic AI writing · shallow content · trend chasing. The human layer provides: operational insight · lived experience · systems thinking · synthesis · narrative depth.
